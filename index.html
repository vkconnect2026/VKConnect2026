General Information

The Organizing and Scientific Committees invite researchers, academics, professionals and students at both graduate and undergraduate levels to submit abstracts for presentation at the “1st Forum on Knowledge Connection 2026: Innovation in the Digital Era - VKConnect2026”. The Forum is designed to facilitate interdisciplinary exchange, strengthen collaborative research between Vietnam and Korea and promote innovation across academic, industrial and societal domains. Accepted submissions will be assigned to either oral or poster presentation sessions.

Conference Tracks

  • Track 1: Saemaul Undong, Governance and Sustainable Development
  • Track 2: Semiconductors, Nanomaterials, and Energy
  • Track 3: Biotechnology for Modern Production
  • Track 4: Environmental Engineering for Green Growth
  • Track 5: Vietnam-Korea Cultural Intersection
  • Track 6: Artificial Intelligence and Big Data Knowledge Connection
  • Track 7: Advanced Biomedicine and Healthcare
  • Track 8: Precision Mechanics for Industry 4.0

Important Dates

Abstract Submission Due: Aug. 15, 2026.
Acceptance Notification: Aug. 22, 2026.
Registration Due: Aug. 22, 2026.
